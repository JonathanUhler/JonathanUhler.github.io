Photography

I wouldn't call myself a photographer, but I enjoy photography. This page is an enumeration of some of my favorite work so far, which I will annotate at some point.

Most of the images taken with a real camera (i.e. not a phone) are unedited. Eventually I would like to stylize all of them and update this site, but that's somewhere down near the end of things I would like to do given the time.

Day-to-day I use an iPhone SE (2nd generation) as my camera. If I buy a new phone in the near future, it will be for the camera. When possible, I also have a Nikon D5000.
